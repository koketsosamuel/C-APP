The Will of God

The Will of God is defined as “what God wants”. For example, saying “the Will of God is not for us to die and go to Hell, but that we may believe in His Son and therefore receive everlasting life” is the same as saying “what God wants is not for us to die and go to Hell, but that we may believe in His Son and therefore receive everlasting life”.

You know sometimes as people we want a lot of things, we want to be educated, we want to marry or be married, we want cars, we want houses, we want children, we want to be healthy, we want to be famous, we want jobs, we want to preach about God, we want to start churches, we want job promotions, we want money and many more things.

But have you ever stopped and asked God what He wants for you, or what He wants from you? If you truly believe that God is your creator, then you must also believe that God knows you more than you know yourself. And if God knows you more than you know yourself then you must also believe that God knows what is best for you, that God knows how to make you happy, God knows what will satisfy you, and that God knows everything you need, even the needs you don’t realize you have.

What God wants is what we should want, let’s forget about what we want, and focus on what God wants for us, as well as what God wants for others, and what God wants for His creations. If we want to live lives of holiness and perfection then let’s forget about everything we want, and everything people want for us and just focus on what God wants. What God wants is best, better than what we would ever suggest.

In the past I wanted to be a computer technician with all my heart, but what God wanted for me was different, it was that I should be His child. And today I am His child and know that being His child is a thousand times better than being anything on earth. Wow, being a child of God is so beautiful that I cried when writing this paragraph, remembering how many times God forgave me just spilled those tears of joy out of my eyes. Wow the Will of God truly is best, and I am a witness. Don’t you want to be a witness too?

Doing the Will of God is amazing, and not doing the Will of God is very dangerous and the Bible provides us with a very very clear example of how dangerous it is to not do the Will of God.

“Not everyone who says to me, ‘Lord, Lord,’ will enter into the kingdom of Heaven – only the one who does the will of my Father in Heaven. On that day, many will say to me, ‘Lord, Lord, didn’t we prophesy in your name, and in your name cast out demons and do many powerful deeds?’ Then I will declare to them, ‘I never knew you. Go away from me, you lawbreakers!’

Matthew 7:21 - 23 (Lord Jesus Christ)

Again I repeat, let’s forget about what we want and what others want, then focus on what God wants and do what God wants.

Pray to God and say “my God, forgive me for all the times I ignored what you want and focused on what I want and what others want, I want to do what you want me to do, I want to dress how you want me to dress, I want to smell how you want me to smell, I want to look how you want me to look, I want to listen to what you want me to listen to, I want to watch what you want me to watch, and I want to be want what you want me to be. Help me, please help me, I want to live by your Will and die by your Will, Amen in the name of Lord Jesus Christ.”
