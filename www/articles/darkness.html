Darkness

We all know what darkness is, it is what causes us to switch on our lights, candles or torches at night. In darkness we do not know the difference between a friend and an enemy, darkness is a state of being absolutely lost, and placing your trust on wrong things and wrong persons. When you are in darkness the devil can come to you and convince you that the Lord Jesus Christ is your enemy. In darkness the devil can convince you that he is God, and you have to praise and worship him, while in return he gives you money, fame, power and assurance that you are living life to the fullest. Yes, a lot of horrible things are possible in darkness, this is the reason why we see many people following false religions like Atheism, Hinduism, Satanism, Islam, Luciferism, Buddhism, and etc.

Darkness is not to be valued, but to be abandoned. Let’s love the Saving light and live in the Saving light, which is our Lord Jesus Christ. To love and have the saving light in your life you must invite the Lord Jesus Christ to come into your life and allow him to dominate your life, because he is the saving light sent into the world by God. If you don’t do this you might end up as an atheist, Buddhist, Muslim, monk, and etc. an example I can give you is found in the false Israelites who claim to be Jews, Israel is not a Jewish country and the reason for this is because they rejected the light and for this reason they perished as they created a new false religion which believes in doctrines of demons which come from the Talmud and other instruments of their new religion which is far from God. Not only did these false Israelites reject the light, but they also reviled Him in a very horrible way that I don’t even feel like I should write their insults against the Son of God for you to see. They are not Jews if they do not submit to the Lord Jesus Christ, although a few do believe in the Lord Jesus Christ, and they are the true Israelites.

The same thing with Islam and every other false religion, they reject the light and sometimes they create their own false Christ so that they can mislead people. If you reject the light you will go to Hell, because you will hate those who love you, misplace your trust, be selfish, be envious and worship idols like money, beauty, fame, power and etc.

Lord Jesus Christ is the saving light of the world sent to us by the Father, so let us live in that light so that we may be able to differentiate between enemies and friends, between lies and the truth, between good and evil, between life and death, between Heaven and Hell, and lastly but very importantly, so that we may be able to know who God is, how to serve him, and who Lord Jesus Christ truly is.

If you have the saving light in your life then you should be grateful to God because without the light we are doomed, but with the saving light we are blessed. Arise, shine, for your light has come, and the glory of Jehovah shines over you.
