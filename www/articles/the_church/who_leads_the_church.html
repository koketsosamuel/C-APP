Who leads the Church?

The Lord Jesus Christ calls Himself as “the Good Shepherd”, and He calls us as His sheep. A Shepherd is someone who takes care of the sheep and leads the sheep. The sheep are lost without the Shepherd, and in our case the Lord Jesus Christ is our Shepherd and we are His sheep. He is our Leader, not anyone else, He is our only Leader and we are all equals.

“ I am the good shepherd. The good shepherd lays down His life for the sheep.

John 10:11 (Lord Jesus Christ)

“But you, do not be called ‘Rabbi,’ for One is your Teacher, the Messiah, and you are all brothers. “And do not call anyone on earth your father, for One is your Father, He who is in the heavens. “ Neither be called leaders, for One is your Leader, the Messiah.

Matthew 23:8 - 10 (Lord Jesus Christ)

The conclusion I can give you about our leader is that our leader is the Lord Jesus Christ and without Him we are completely lost, just like sheep without a shepherd, without Him we are in darkness because He is our Light! And this is my final conclusion because the verses I quoted are where the Lord Jesus Christ confirms what am saying, so arguing with me means that you are also arguing with the Lord Jesus Christ because what am telling you comes from Him.

“Everyone who hears these words of mine and does them is like a wise man who built his house on rock. The rain fell, the flood came, and the winds beat against that house, but it did not collapse because its foundation had been laid on rock.

Matthew 7:24 - 25 (Lord Jesus Christ)

You will agree with me if you have laid your foundation on the rock!
